About

I love weddings and everything about them! This is my passion and I promise to take great care of this big day for you. I’m passionate, organized, detail oriented and conscientious. I graduated from college in 2012 with a degree in Communications and have my Wedding Planning Certificate. Once I graduated I took on a position at a prominent resort in the Lehigh Valley where I had detailed insight into the event industry for 5 years. It is my goal to make your wedding day better than you could have ever imagined. An interesting fact about me is that I have an addiction to pickles and love anything with the flavor of salt and vinegar. When I’m not coordinating a wedding I enjoy working out, reading, baking, spending time with loved ones and my cute Daschund named Otter. A beloved hobby of mine is traveling and getting to experience new adventures! My other passion is being around kids. I enjoy being a family's nanny and serving in the children’s ministry at my church. My favorite part of a wedding is watching the groom’s reaction to his bride walking down the aisle to him. I always tear up!
